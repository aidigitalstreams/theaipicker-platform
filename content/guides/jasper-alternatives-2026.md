## FAQ

**Is Jasper still worth it in 2026?**
For marketing teams that produce high-volume, brand-consistent content across multiple channels, yes. Jasper's campaign workflows and brand voice training are genuinely useful for coordinated marketing. For solo creators and small teams, cheaper alternatives deliver comparable results.

**Can ChatGPT replace Jasper?**
For most individual users, yes. ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) handles content generation, brainstorming, and editing at less than half Jasper's price. The main gap is brand voice training and marketing-specific templates.

**What's the cheapest Jasper alternative?**
Grammarly at $12/month (for editing) or ChatGPT/Claude at $20/month (for generation). Both are under half of Jasper's $49/month starting price.
