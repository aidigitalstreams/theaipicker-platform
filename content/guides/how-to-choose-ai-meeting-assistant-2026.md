## Three Tools, Three Strengths

### "I want the simplest, most generous option" → Fathom (86/100)

Fathom does one thing exceptionally: it joins your meetings and delivers clear, accurate summaries with action items. The free plan is unlimited. The setup takes minutes. There is almost no learning curve.

**Best for:** Individuals and small teams who want meeting notes without complexity or cost.

### "I want my team collaborating during meetings" → Otter (84/100)

Otter's live transcription appears in real-time during meetings. Team members can highlight text, add comments, and flag action items as the meeting progresses — turning meetings into collaborative documents.

**Best for:** Teams that want to actively work with the transcript during the meeting itself, not just review it afterwards.

### "I need meeting data flowing into our CRM" → Fireflies (83/100)

Fireflies integrates deeply with Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Asana, ClickUp, and Slack. Meeting summaries, action items, and conversation intelligence (talk ratios, sentiment, topic tracking) flow into your existing tools automatically.

**Best for:** Sales teams, customer success teams, and any team whose meeting data needs to feed into business workflows.
