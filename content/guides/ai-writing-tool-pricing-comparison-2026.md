## Which One Is Right for Your Budget?

**If you can spend £0:** Start with Copy.ai's free tier (2,000 words/month is enough for most side projects). If you want more tools in one package, Rytr free tier gives you 10,000 chars/month but with a lower ceiling.

**If you can spend up to $10/month:** Rytr Saver. Best per-dollar value in the category.

**If you can spend up to $25/month:** Writesonic Individual for SEO-focused writers, or Copy.ai Starter annual billing (~$36/mo — so just above this band).

**If you can spend up to $50/month:** Copy.ai Starter ($49/mo unlimited words) is the strongest single pick. Jasper Creator at $49/mo if brand-voice quality is the priority.

**If you can spend up to $100/month:** Writesonic Standard at $99/mo gives you 1M words + full SEO suite. Jasper Pro at $69+ if brand-voice teams matter.

**If you can spend over $100/month:** Look at Business tiers — all five vendors negotiate custom pricing for teams. Get quotes from two or three before committing.

## The Value Traps to Avoid

**"Unlimited" is not always unlimited.** Several tools advertise unlimited words on their Starter tier but meter advanced workflows separately. Read the small print before heavy use.

**Annual discounts are genuine but lock you in.** Most tools offer 15-25% off annual billing. Worth it if you're confident in the tool — risky if you might switch vendors in 3-6 months.

**Trial ≠ free plan.** Jasper and Anyword do not offer a true free plan. Their "free" options are 7-day trials. If you need ongoing free access, stick to Copy.ai, Rytr, or Writesonic.

**The entry price is rarely the real price.** Most heavy writers hit caps and upgrade within weeks. Plan for the likely upgrade tier, not the headline entry price.

## FAQ

**Which AI writing tool has the best free tier?**
Copy.ai — 2,000 words/month with unlimited generations and no time limit. Rytr is second with 10,000 chars/month (roughly 1,400 words).

**Is Jasper worth $49/month when Copy.ai gives unlimited words at the same price?**
For most individual writers, no. For teams using brand-voice governance across multiple users, Jasper's structure is still stronger. Read both the [Jasper](/jasper-review/) and [Copy.ai](/copy-ai-review/) reviews before committing.

**What's the cheapest paid AI writing tool?**
Rytr Saver at $9/month is the cheapest paid entry in the mainstream category.

**Do any of these tools offer lifetime deals?**
Not in the mainstream pricing we tracked through early 2026. Some tools occasionally run AppSumo lifetime deals but they are sporadic and not bookable.

**Can I use the free tiers for commercial work?**
All five tools allow commercial use on their paid tiers. Free tier commercial use is typically allowed but always verify in the tool's current terms before publishing.
