## Quick Recommendations

**For hero images, editorial, illustration, concept art →** Midjourney. Still the aesthetic ceiling.

**For complex prompt adherence or you already pay for ChatGPT →** DALL-E 3. Best literal following; bundled with ChatGPT Plus.

**For iterative work, series, custom training, tight budgets →** Leonardo AI. Free tier worth testing; Artisan at $24/mo (annual) is the working-creator sweet spot.
