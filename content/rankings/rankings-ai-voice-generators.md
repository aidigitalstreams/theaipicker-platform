## Quick Takes

**Best overall:** ElevenLabs takes the top spot with the highest output quality (96/100) and largest voice library in the category. Voice cloning is accessible from the $5/mo Starter tier — every other tool here either gates cloning behind enterprise pricing or charges significantly more.

**Best for video creators:** Lovo AI's built-in video editor and 100+ language coverage make it the strongest single-tool workflow for YouTube automation and course production. You get voice generation, video assembly, and subtitles in one platform.

**Best for presentations and corporate workflows:** Murf AI's native integrations with Canva, PowerPoint and Google Slides win it the corporate use case. Full SSML support also gives finer pronunciation control than competitors.

**Best for reading-to-listening:** Speechify is the easiest tool here for converting books, articles and PDFs into audio. The premium voices are excellent and the consumer-facing app is the most polished. Biometric Identity Locking on voice cloning is a unique privacy feature.
