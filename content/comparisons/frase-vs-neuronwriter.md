## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Content Optimisation

Both tools analyse top-ranking content and provide term recommendations for your target keyword. Frase's editor is cleaner and more intuitive — the SERP analysis is presented alongside the writing area, with clear scoring and term suggestions. The AI writer generates reasonable first drafts from the brief.

NeuronWriter's optimisation is competent but the interface is less polished. The content scoring works, but the UX requires more clicks to access the same information Frase surfaces inline.

**Winner: Frase** — better editor, smoother workflow.

### Research & Briefs

Frase excels here. The research tools pull in competitor content, generate comprehensive briefs with headings, questions, and statistics, and present SERP analysis in a format that's immediately actionable. The "Questions" feature pulls People Also Ask data that's useful for FAQ sections.

NeuronWriter focuses more narrowly on NLP term analysis and competitor heading structure. The research is functional but narrower in scope.

**Winner: Frase** — broader, more actionable research tools.
