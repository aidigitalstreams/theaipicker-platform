## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Image Quality

Midjourney's output quality is the benchmark for the category. Images are consistently beautiful, with strong composition, coherent lighting, and an artistic quality that competitors struggle to match. The aesthetic "Midjourney look" is recognisable and desirable.

Leonardo produces strong images but with more variance in quality. Top-tier Leonardo outputs match Midjourney; average outputs are a step below. Leonardo's strength is variety — different model presets produce different aesthetic styles.

**Winner: Midjourney** (more consistent quality)

### Creative Toolkit

Leonardo offers a significantly broader creative toolkit: inpainting (edit specific areas), outpainting (extend images beyond borders), image-to-image transformation, canvas editing, and custom model training. These features turn Leonardo from a generator into a creative platform.

Midjourney focuses on generation. Editing capabilities are more limited — you can upscale, create variations, and use region-specific regeneration, but the editing toolkit is narrower.

**Winner: Leonardo** (significantly broader toolkit)

### Ease of Use

Leonardo has a clean web interface accessible from any browser. No Discord required. The UI is intuitive with clear model selection, parameter controls, and editing tools.

Midjourney's Discord interface has a learning curve — prompting through chat commands is not intuitive for new users. The web interface improves this significantly but is still less polished than Leonardo's purpose-built UI.

**Winner: Leonardo** (more accessible, especially for non-technical users)

### Free Plan

Leonardo offers a generous free tier with daily token credits — enough to generate a meaningful number of images per day. Midjourney does not offer a free plan — you must subscribe to generate images.

**Winner: Leonardo** (free plan vs. no free plan)

### Custom Model Training

Leonardo allows users to train custom models on their own image sets — useful for consistent brand imagery, character consistency, and style replication. Midjourney does not offer custom model training.

**Winner: Leonardo** (unique feature)

### Commercial Licensing

Both include commercial licensing on paid plans. Midjourney requires a paid subscription for any use; Leonardo's free-tier images have usage restrictions.

**Winner: Tie** (both include commercial rights on paid plans)
