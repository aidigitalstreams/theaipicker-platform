## Head-to-Head Comparison

### AI Image Quality

Recraft produces more refined AI-generated images — cleaner compositions, more accurate text rendering, and style consistency across generations. The vector SVG output is unique to Recraft and genuinely useful for professional design work. The canvas interface allows direct refinement of generated assets.

Canva's Magic Media (text-to-image) produces functional but less polished images. The strength isn't individual image quality — it's the breadth of AI tools. Magic Studio includes background removal, image expansion, text effects, animation, and 20+ other AI-powered features.

**Winner: Recraft** for individual image quality, **Canva** for breadth of AI tools.

### Design Ecosystem

Canva is the more complete design platform by a wide margin. Templates, brand kits, team collaboration, presentation creation, video editing, social media scheduling, and print services — all in one platform. AI features enhance an already comprehensive ecosystem.

Recraft is focused specifically on AI asset generation. The Figma-like canvas is excellent for creating and refining individual design assets, but it doesn't offer the template library, team features, or format variety that Canva provides.

**Winner: Canva** — the broader ecosystem is hard to match.

## Our Recommendation

**Most designers should use Canva for daily work and Recraft when AI asset quality matters.** Canva covers 90% of design needs with its template library and AI tools.
