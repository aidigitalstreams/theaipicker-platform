## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Unique Strengths

**Recraft** is the only AI tool that generates clean vector SVGs, renders text accurately in images, and maintains brand consistency across multiple generations. The canvas interface allows direct editing of generated assets. These capabilities make it genuinely unique — no other tool in the category produces usable SVG output.

**Leonardo** offers the most generous free tier in AI image generation (150 tokens/day, ~20-30 images), strong photorealistic output approaching Midjourney quality, image-to-image capabilities, and a canvas mode for regional editing. The token refresh model means regular daily use is sustainable without paying.

**Winner: Depends entirely on use case** — they serve different needs.

### Image Quality

Leonardo produces stronger photorealistic imagery — landscapes, portraits, product shots, and scene compositions are closer to Midjourney's quality. The latest models handle complex compositions and lighting well.

Recraft produces stronger design assets — illustrations, icons, branded graphics, and typography-integrated images. The style consistency across multiple generations is unmatched. For raster photorealism, Leonardo wins. For design-purpose imagery, Recraft wins.

**Winner: Leonardo** for photorealism, **Recraft** for design assets.
