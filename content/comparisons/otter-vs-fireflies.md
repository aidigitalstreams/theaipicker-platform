## Otter Overview

Otter.ai is an AI meeting assistant focused on real-time transcription and collaboration. Its signature feature is live transcription during meetings — participants see the transcript building in real-time and can highlight, comment, and add action items as the meeting progresses. Otter integrates with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams, sending a bot to join and transcribe meetings automatically.

Our full review scores Otter **84/100**.

## Fireflies Overview

Fireflies.ai is an AI meeting assistant focused on post-meeting intelligence. It transcribes meetings, generates AI summaries and action items, and integrates deeply with CRM and project management tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, Notion, Asana, Slack). The conversation intelligence features — tracking talk-to-listen ratios, sentiment, topics discussed — make it particularly strong for sales and customer success teams.

## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Real-Time Collaboration

Otter's live transcription experience is the best in the category. During meetings, participants can view the transcript in real-time, add highlights, insert comments, and flag action items. This turns meetings into collaborative documents rather than recordings you process later.

Fireflies focuses on post-meeting processing. Its real-time capabilities are more limited.

**Winner: Otter** (significant advantage for in-meeting use)

### Post-Meeting Automation

Fireflies excels at what happens after the meeting ends. Automatic summaries, action item extraction, and deep integrations with CRM tools (Salesforce, HubSpot) and project management tools (Notion, Asana, ClickUp) turn meeting transcripts into actionable workflows.

Otter offers summaries and action items but with less integration depth for post-meeting automation.

**Winner: Fireflies** (stronger CRM and project management integrations)

### Conversation Intelligence

Fireflies provides analytics across your team's meetings: talk-to-listen ratios, sentiment tracking, topic extraction, and custom trackers for specific keywords or phrases. Sales teams use this to analyse call performance and identify coaching opportunities.

Otter offers basic analytics but less depth in conversation intelligence.

**Winner: Fireflies** (purpose-built for team analytics)

### Transcription Accuracy

Both tools produce accurate transcriptions on clean audio with clear speakers. Both struggle with heavy accents, crosstalk, and poor audio quality — a category-wide limitation. In controlled comparisons, accuracy is close enough that other factors matter more than transcription quality alone.

**Winner: Tie**
