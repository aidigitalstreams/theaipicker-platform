## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Free Plan

Fathom's free plan is the best in the category: unlimited recordings, unlimited AI summaries, and core features with no time or meeting caps. Otter's free plan offers 300 minutes/month with a 30-minute per-meeting limit.

For users evaluating both tools, Fathom lets you use it indefinitely for free. Otter's free tier is a trial that nudges you toward paid plans.

**Winner: Fathom** (significantly more generous)

### Summary Quality

Both produce strong AI summaries. Fathom's summaries tend to be more concise and action-oriented — users consistently praise the clarity. Otter's summaries are slightly more detailed but can include more noise.

**Winner: Fathom** (marginal advantage in clarity and conciseness)

### Real-Time Collaboration

Otter's live transcription with in-meeting comments, highlights, and shared action items is a feature Fathom does not match. If your team collaborates on meeting notes in real-time during the meeting itself, Otter is the only choice.

Fathom focuses on post-meeting delivery — summaries appear after the meeting ends, not during.

**Winner: Otter** (unique feature, no equivalent in Fathom)

### Ease of Use

Fathom is designed around simplicity. Install it, join meetings, get summaries. There is almost no configuration needed. Otter has more features but also more complexity — settings, permissions, team management, and integration configuration.

**Winner: Fathom** (deliberately simpler)

### Integrations

Otter integrates with a broader range of tools and offers deeper enterprise features. Fathom integrates with CRM tools (Salesforce, HubSpot) and Slack but with less depth overall.

**Winner: Otter** (broader integration ecosystem)

### Pricing Comparison

Fathom's free tier is so generous that many users never need to pay. Otter's paid plans unlock features the free tier restricts.
