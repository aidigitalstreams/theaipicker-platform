## H1: Affiliate Disclosure

The AI Picker is reader-supported. When you click one of our links and sign up for a product, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

### How It Works

Many AI tool companies run affiliate programmes. When we link to a tool and you sign up through that link, the company pays us a small percentage of your subscription as a referral fee. The price you pay is exactly the same — whether you use our link or go directly to the site.

### How It Affects Our Reviews

It doesn't.

Our recommendations are based entirely on our research and analysis of each tool. We will never change a rating, adjust a recommendation, or promote a tool because of its commission rate. If we did, you'd stop trusting us — and trust is the only thing that makes this site worth visiting.

We will always recommend tools with lower or no commissions over tools with higher ones, if the lower-commission tool is simply better. You'll find articles where we recommend free alternatives over paid tools — even though free tools earn us nothing.

### How to Support Us

If you find our reviews helpful and decide to try a tool we've recommended, using our links is the best way to support The AI Picker. It costs you nothing extra and helps us keep researching and reviewing new tools.

If you'd prefer not to use our links, that's completely fine too. We'd rather you find the right tool than click the right link.

If you have any questions about how we make money or how it relates to our reviews, **[get in touch](/contact/)**. We're happy to be transparent about all of it.
