## H1: Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [DATE — to be set at launch]

The AI Picker ("we", "us", "our") operates the website theaipicker.com. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

We believe in collecting as little data as possible. If we don't need it, we don't collect it.

### What We Collect

**Analytics Data (via Google Analytics)**
When you visit our site, Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data including: pages visited, time on site, device type, browser type, approximate geographic region, and referral source. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

**Contact Form Submissions**
If you use our contact form, we collect the information you provide (typically your name and email address). We use this only to respond to your message and do not add you to any mailing list.

**Cookies**
We use essential cookies to make the site function properly and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

### Affiliate Links

When you click an affiliate link on our site, you are redirected to a third-party website. That company may use their own cookies to track that you came from The AI Picker. This is how they attribute the referral. We do not control what data those third-party sites collect — please refer to their own privacy policies.

### What We Don't Do

- We don't sell your data to anyone
- We don't run targeted advertising
- We don't build user profiles
- We don't use retargeting pixels
- We don't collect payment information (all transactions happen on third-party sites)

### Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK-based site, we comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation. You have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of any data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your data
- **Restriction** — ask us to limit how we use your data
- **Objection** — object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [EMAIL — to be set at launch].

### Data Storage & Security

Any data we hold (contact form submissions) is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary. Analytics data is held by Google under their own data retention policies.
