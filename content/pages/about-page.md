## H1: About The AI Picker

We started The AI Picker because we were tired of AI tool "reviews" that read like they were written by the tool's own marketing team.

You know the ones. They list every feature from the pricing page, declare the tool "great for beginners and professionals alike," and slap an affiliate link at the bottom. No real opinion. No genuine analysis. No help.

We wanted something better.

### What We Actually Do

We research AI tools thoroughly — digging into official documentation, real user reviews, community feedback, pricing structures, and competitive comparisons. We do the legwork so you don't have to.

If a tool is worth paying for, we tell you why and for whom. If a tool is overpriced, underpowered, or just not as good as the alternative, we tell you that too. Every tool is scored out of 100 across five weighted factors, so our ratings are consistent and comparable across every category.

### How We Make Money

We're transparent about this: we earn a commission when you click one of our affiliate links and sign up for a tool. This costs you nothing extra — the price is the same whether you use our link or not.

But here's what matters: we never recommend a tool because the commission is good. We recommend tools because they're good. If a tool with a 50% commission is worse than a tool with a 10% commission, we recommend the better tool. Every time.

You can read our full [affiliate disclosure](/affiliate-disclosure/) for the details.

### Why Trust Us?

We don't accept sponsored reviews. We don't let tool companies preview our articles before we publish. We don't change our ratings because someone offered a better commission.

Every claim in our reviews can be backed up by research — and we'll never overstate what we know.
