## H1: How We Review AI Tools

Every review on The AI Picker follows the same process. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

### Step 1: We Research Thoroughly

We dig into everything available about each tool — official documentation, user reviews across multiple platforms, community feedback on Reddit and forums, expert opinions, and real-world case studies. We don't skim the features page and call it a review. We analyse pricing structures, compare feature sets against competitors, and look for the details that most review sites skip.

### Step 2: We Compare Fairly

Every review includes context. How does this tool compare to the obvious alternatives? Is it worth the price difference? We maintain consistent scoring criteria across every tool in the same category so our comparisons are meaningful.

Each tool category has its own set of comparison data points — the specific features and capabilities that matter for that type of tool. An AI writing tool is compared on different criteria than an AI voice generator, because the things that matter are different. This means our comparison tables show you what's actually relevant, not generic feature lists.

### Step 3: We Score Consistently

Every tool is rated out of 100 based on five weighted factors:

| Factor | Weight | What We're Looking At |
|--------|--------|-----------------------|
| **Core Performance** | 30% | Does it do its main job well? |
| **Ease of Use** | 20% | Can a non-technical user figure it out quickly? |
| **Value for Money** | 25% | Is the price fair for what you get? |
| **Output Quality** | 15% | How good is the end result compared to competitors? |
| **Support & Reliability** | 10% | Does it work consistently? Is help available when needed? |

The 100-point scale means we can show meaningful differences between tools. An 82 versus a 77 tells a clearer story than an 8 versus an 8. Every review shows the full breakdown — never just the final number.

### Step 4: We Write What We Think

Our reviews aren't summaries of the features page. They're our honest assessment — what stood out, what fell short, what surprised us, and who we'd recommend it to.

We always pick a winner in comparisons. "It depends" isn't a review — it's a cop-out. We tell you what we'd choose and why, then explain when the other option might be the better fit.

### What We Don't Do

- **Accept sponsored reviews.** Nobody pays for placement on this site.
- **Let companies preview articles.** Our reviews are published as-is.
- **Change ratings for higher commissions.** We recommend what's best, not what pays best.

### How You Can Use Our Data

Beyond individual reviews, we maintain a **Category Leaderboard** for each type of AI tool — a living, ranked table of every tool we've reviewed in that category, ordered by score. These leaderboards update whenever we publish or revise a review, so they always reflect current data.

We also offer an **Interactive Comparison Builder** where you can select 3-5 tools from the same category and view them side-by-side, comparing scores and category-specific features in one table. It's designed to help you make a decision quickly using the data we've already gathered.

### Keeping Reviews Current

AI tools change fast. A tool that was brilliant six months ago might have raised prices, cut features, or been overtaken by a competitor.

We revisit our published reviews regularly and update them when things change. Every article shows when it was last updated so you know you're reading current information. When a score changes, the category leaderboard updates automatically.
