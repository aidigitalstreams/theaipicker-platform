## FAQ

**Are free AI tools actually good enough?**
For most individual users, yes. The free tiers listed above handle daily productivity tasks, writing assistance, research, and content creation at a level that would have cost hundreds per month two years ago. Paid tiers add speed, capacity, and specialist features — but the free versions do the core job.

**What's the catch with free AI tools?**
Usage limits (daily or monthly caps), reduced model access (smaller or slower AI models), and feature restrictions (no team collaboration, no API access). None of these tools sell your data as the "free tier catch" — the free plans are genuine user acquisition strategies funded by paid subscribers.

**When should I upgrade to paid?**
When the free tier's limits regularly interrupt your workflow. If you hit ChatGPT's rate limit daily, upgrade. If 150 Leonardo tokens aren't enough, upgrade. If 300 Otter minutes run out mid-month, upgrade. Don't pay until the free tier proves it's a tool you actually use.
