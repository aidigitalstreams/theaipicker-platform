## A Note on Academic Integrity

These tools are meant to assist your learning, not replace it. Using AI to generate essays you submit as your own work is academic misconduct at most institutions. The best use of AI tools for students is research assistance (finding and verifying sources), writing improvement (grammar, clarity, structure), concept understanding (explanations and practice questions), and productivity (transcription, presentations, organisation). Check your institution's AI use policy before using these tools for assessed work.

## FAQ

**Are AI tools cheating for students?**
Using AI for research, editing, and study assistance is generally accepted. Submitting AI-generated work as your own is academic misconduct. The line varies by institution — check your specific policies.

**What's the best completely free AI tool for students?**
Perplexity for research, ChatGPT for general assistance, Grammarly for writing quality, and Otter for lecture transcription. All four have genuinely usable free tiers.

**Should students pay for AI tools?**
For most students, the free tiers are sufficient. If you write extensively, Grammarly Premium ($12/month) or QuillBot Premium (~£3.33/month) are the most affordable meaningful upgrades.
