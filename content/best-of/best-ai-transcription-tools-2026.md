## 4. Descript — Best for Creators

Descript reimagines transcription by making it the editing interface. Transcribe audio or video, then edit the content by editing the text — delete a sentence from the transcript and the corresponding audio/video is removed. This text-based editing paradigm is revolutionary for podcasters and video creators. Overdub (AI voice cloning) lets you fix mistakes by typing corrections. The tradeoff is complexity — Descript is a full editing suite, not just a transcription tool.

## FAQ

**What is the most accurate AI transcription tool?**
For pure AI transcription, Sonix and Otter lead on accuracy. For human-verified transcription, Rev's 99%+ accuracy is unmatched.

**Is AI transcription good enough for legal use?**
AI-only transcription (90-95% accuracy) is not sufficient for legal proceedings. Rev's human-verified service (99%+) is used by legal firms when accuracy is critical.

**Can AI transcription tools handle multiple speakers?**
Yes. Sonix, Otter, and Trint all offer automatic speaker identification (diarisation). Accuracy varies with audio quality and number of speakers.
