## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best AI video tool in 2026?**
For generative video, Runway (85/100). For avatar video, HeyGen (82/100). For enterprise multilingual avatar video, Synthesia (79/100). For L&D scenario training, Colossyan (78/100). For script-to-video and long-form-to-shorts production, Pictory (76/100). The right answer depends entirely on the job.

**Is Runway better than Synthesia?**
They solve different problems. Runway is a generative video tool for creators. Synthesia is an avatar-based video tool for corporate communications. Comparing them directly is less useful than asking "what kind of video am I making?"

**What is the cheapest AI video tool?**
Pictory Starter at $19/month (annual) is the lowest sticker price in the list, though the realistic tier for working use is Professional at $39/month. Runway Standard at $12/month is the cheapest way into Gen-4 generative video.

**Do AI video tools include commercial licensing?**
All five tools in this list include commercial licensing on their paid plans. Free tiers are generally non-commercial. Always verify current licensing on each provider's official site before publishing.

**Which AI video tool is best for beginners?**
For beginners, Pictory (easiest workflow, lowest learning curve) or HeyGen's free tier (for avatar-based video). Runway has the steepest learning curve but the highest ceiling.
