## How to Choose the Right AI Writing Tool

The honest truth is that the "best" tool depends on three questions:

**1. What's your budget?**
- **Under $15/mo:** Rytr is the only realistic pick on this list
- **$15-30/mo:** Copy.ai (unlimited words, balanced quality)
- **$30+/mo:** Anyword, Writesonic, or Jasper depending on use case

**2. What are you writing most?**
- **Short-form (emails, social, ads):** Rytr or Copy.ai
- **Long-form blog content:** Copy.ai or Jasper
- **SEO articles:** Writesonic
- **Paid-ad copy:** Anyword
- **Brand-consistent marketing copy at scale:** Jasper

**3. Do you have a specialist need?**
- **Predicting which copy converts:** Anyword's Predictive Performance Score is uniquely useful
- **SEO scoring while writing:** Writesonic's built-in optimisation
- **Brand voice training:** Jasper (best-in-class) or Anyword
- **Multi-language at low cost:** Rytr (30+ languages on $9/mo)

If you don't have a clear specialist need, **start with Rytr if budget is tight** ($9/mo, free plan, easy learning curve) and **Copy.ai if budget is moderate** ($24/mo, unlimited generations, balanced quality). Both have free plans — try them on real work before committing.

[Try Rytr Free →](https://rytr.me) is the easiest way to test whether AI writing fits your workflow at all — under £10/month, no risk.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What's the best AI writing tool for blogs?

For long-form blog content with good SEO support, **Writesonic** is the strongest pick because of built-in SEO scoring and plagiarism checking. For long-form blog content prioritising output quality and brand consistency, **Jasper** wins. For balanced quality at unlimited word counts, **Copy.ai** is the best value.

### What's the cheapest AI writing tool?

**Rytr at $9/month** is the cheapest serious AI writing tool in 2026, and it includes unlimited generations, 40+ templates, and 30+ languages. The free tier (10,000 characters/month with commercial rights) is genuinely useful before paying.

### Which AI writing tool has the best free plan?

**Rytr's free plan** is the most generous — 10,000 characters per month with full template and tone access plus commercial rights. **Copy.ai's free plan** offers 2,000 words. **Writesonic's free plan** gives 25 generations. Anyword and Jasper offer trials only, no permanent free tier.

### Is Jasper worth the higher price?

Jasper is worth the price if **brand consistency at scale** is core to your work — marketing teams, agencies, or content-heavy businesses where Brand Voice training pays back the subscription. For solo creators or small businesses, **Copy.ai or Rytr deliver 80-90% of the output quality at a fraction of the price**.

### Can I use AI writing tools commercially?

Yes — every tool on this list includes commercial rights on its paid plans. Rytr includes commercial rights even on the free plan. Always check current terms before publishing client work, but commercial use is the standard expectation for paid AI writing subscriptions.
