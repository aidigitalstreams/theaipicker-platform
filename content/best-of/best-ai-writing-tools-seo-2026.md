The Content Editor is the core feature: paste your draft and Surfer highlights what's missing compared to pages that currently rank. The Audit tool analyses published pages and suggests specific improvements.

**What we liked:** The content scoring is the most granular available — it goes beyond keyword counting into NLP entity coverage that reflects how Google understands topics. ## FAQ

**Do I need an SEO writing tool if I use ChatGPT?**
ChatGPT can write content, but it doesn't analyse what's currently ranking for your target keyword. SEO writing tools provide real-time competitive intelligence — what topics to cover, what terms to include, how long your article should be — based on actual SERP data. They're complementary: use ChatGPT for drafting, an SEO tool for optimisation.

**Is Surfer SEO worth $99/month?**
For professional content teams producing 10+ articles per month and targeting competitive keywords, yes. The content scoring and SERP analysis save hours of manual research per article. For solo bloggers producing 2-4 articles monthly, Frase at $15/month covers the essential features.

**Can I do SEO without these tools?**
Yes — manual SERP analysis (reading top-ranking pages, identifying patterns, covering similar topics) achieves the same result. These tools automate and quantify what experienced SEO writers do intuitively. They're productivity tools, not magic.
