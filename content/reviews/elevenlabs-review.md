## What Is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is an AI-powered text-to-speech platform that converts written text into remarkably natural-sounding audio. Founded in 2022, it's quickly become the most talked-about name in AI voice generation, and for good reason — the quality gap between ElevenLabs and most competitors is immediately obvious when you hear the output.

The platform offers text-to-speech in 74 languages, instant voice cloning from short audio samples, professional voice cloning for studio-grade results, and an AI dubbing studio that can localise videos across 32 languages. It serves everyone from solo YouTubers to enterprise media companies, with pricing tiers to match.

## Key Features

**Voice Quality That Sets the Standard.** ElevenLabs' latest model, Eleven v3, produces voices with genuine emotional range. Based on our research, users consistently describe the output as the most natural-sounding TTS available. The v3 model introduces audio tags for emotional control — you can specify tones like curious, excited, or sombre, and the output adjusts accordingly. This isn't gimmick territory; it meaningfully changes how the final audio sounds.

**Voice Cloning — Two Tiers.** Instant Voice Cloning creates a usable custom voice from as little as 30 seconds of audio, available from the Starter plan (£5/month). Professional Voice Cloning, available on the Creator plan (£22/month) and above, uses around 30 minutes of high-quality audio to create hyper-realistic voice models. The instant option is significantly faster than competitors like Murf AI, which restricts voice cloning to enterprise plans entirely.

**Massive Voice Library.** Over 5,000 pre-made voices across 70+ languages, plus 10,000+ community-created clones in the Voice Library marketplace. Finding a voice that fits your project is rarely a problem.

**AI Dubbing Studio.** Upload a video and ElevenLabs will dub it into any of 32 languages, preserving the original speaker's emotion, timing, and tone. It supports YouTube, TikTok, and Vimeo URLs, or direct file uploads up to 500MB and 45 minutes. The speaker detection handles multiple voices, even with overlapping dialogue.

**Streaming API.** For developers, the streaming API delivers audio with latency as low as 75ms on Flash models. SDKs are available for Python and JavaScript, making integration into custom workflows straightforward.

## What We Didn't Like

**Credits run out faster than you'd expect.** The credit-per-character system means longer scripts burn through your allocation quickly. A 10-minute voiceover script can easily consume 15,000-20,000 credits, which is more than half the Starter plan's monthly allowance.

**Pricing jumps are steep.** The gap between Creator (£22) and Pro (£99) is significant. Users who outgrow the Creator plan's 100,000 credits face a nearly 5x price increase to get to the next tier.

**SSML support is inconsistent.** The latest v3 model doesn't support SSML break tags at all, which is frustrating for users who rely on fine-grained pronunciation and pacing control. You have to use older models for full SSML support.

**Legacy voice deprecation burned existing users.** ElevenLabs deprecated legacy voices in early 2026, forcing users to rebuild workflows. This kind of platform change erodes trust with long-term subscribers.
