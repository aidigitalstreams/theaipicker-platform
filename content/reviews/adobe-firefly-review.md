## What Is Adobe Firefly?

Adobe Firefly is the generative AI model family developed by Adobe. The current image generation model is Firefly Image 3 (with Firefly Image 4 in iterative rollout). Firefly also powers vector generation, text effects, generative fill, generative expand, and a growing video-generation capability.

Firefly's defining positioning is commercial safety. Unlike Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion derivatives — which have been trained on datasets including copyrighted web imagery — Firefly is trained exclusively on Adobe Stock, openly licensed, and public domain content. Adobe offers indemnification on commercial use of Firefly-generated content, which is meaningful for marketing teams, agencies, and enterprises where image provenance is a real legal concern.

The product is accessible three ways: firefly.adobe.com as a standalone web app, inside Adobe Creative Cloud apps (Photoshop Generative Fill is the most widely used feature), and inside Adobe Express for designers who do not use the full Creative Cloud stack.

## Key Features

**Text-to-image generation.** The core. Prompt-based image generation at web-resolution output. Quality is good; aesthetic ceiling is below Midjourney.

**Generative Fill (Photoshop).** The killer feature. Select a region in Photoshop, prompt a change, and Firefly generates a seamless fill. This is how most Firefly use happens in practice — through Photoshop, not the standalone app.

**Generative Expand.** Extend an image beyond its original canvas. Integrated into Photoshop and available standalone.

**Text-to-Vector (Illustrator).** Generate editable vector graphics from prompts inside Illustrator. Unique among mainstream generators — competitors produce rasters.

**Text Effects.** Apply AI-generated textures and effects to typography. Useful for marketing and design.

**Generative video (growing).** Firefly Video model is rolling out with text-to-video and image-to-video generation. Quality is competent; the workflow is strongest inside Premiere Enhance rather than the standalone app.

**Commercial indemnification.** Adobe indemnifies enterprise customers against IP claims on Firefly outputs. This is not a marketing line — it is a meaningful legal posture that no competitor matches.

**Integration across Adobe apps.** Photoshop, Illustrator, Express, Premiere, Lightroom. The Firefly capability is woven into tools creative professionals already use.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Adobe Firefly better than Midjourney?**
Different fit. Midjourney wins on aesthetic ceiling; Firefly wins on commercial safety, Creative Cloud integration, and vector generation. Many creative pros use both.

**Is Firefly free?**
Yes, the free tier includes limited generative credits and outputs are watermarked. Premium at $9.99/month removes watermarks and adds 2,000 credits/month.

**Can I use Firefly images commercially?**
Yes. Paid-tier outputs are licensed for commercial use and Adobe provides indemnification on enterprise plans — a category-unique legal backing.

**Does Firefly work in Photoshop?**
Yes. Photoshop Generative Fill is powered by Firefly and is included with Photoshop / Creative Cloud subscriptions.

**Is Firefly trained on copyrighted content?**
No. Firefly is trained on Adobe Stock, openly licensed, and public domain content — the commercial-safety positioning is based on this training approach.
