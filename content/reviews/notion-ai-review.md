## What Is Notion AI?

Notion is an all-in-one workspace tool — a hybrid of document editor, project manager, wiki, and database — used by millions of teams and individuals to organise information, manage projects, and collaborate. Notion AI is the artificial intelligence layer integrated directly into this workspace, capable of writing, summarising, searching, and — since the September 2025 Notion 3.0 update — autonomously executing multi-step tasks.

What differentiates Notion AI from tools like Jasper, ChatGPT, or Grammarly is context. Those tools work with the text you give them. Notion AI works with everything in your workspace — every document, database, meeting note, project board, and comment — and uses that organisational knowledge to provide answers and complete tasks that would be impossible for a standalone AI. It's the difference between an AI assistant you brief from scratch every time and one that already knows your business.

## Key Features

**Workspace Q&A (Ask Notion).** Ask natural language questions about your workspace content. "What were the action items from the product sync last week?" "What's the current status of the Q3 roadmap?" "Summarise all customer feedback about pricing." Notion AI searches across all your pages, databases, and shared content to provide contextual answers with source citations.

**AI Agents.** Launched with Notion 3.0 in September 2025. Autonomous AI agents that work for up to 20 minutes, performing multi-step tasks across hundreds of pages simultaneously. Build project plans, compile feedback from multiple sources, update database entries at scale, draft follow-ups based on meeting notes. This is the most sophisticated agentic capability built into any workspace tool.

**Writing Assistant.** Invoked with the spacebar on any page. Summarise, improve writing, change tone, translate (16+ languages), draft from scratch, brainstorm ideas, create action items, and generate outlines. Functional for everyday writing tasks within the Notion editor.

**Database Autofill.** Automatically classify database entries, summarise content, extract tags, generate descriptions, suggest priorities, and draft follow-ups. Turns manual database management into an AI-assisted process.

**Translation.** Real-time translation of page content into 16+ languages. Useful for international teams working in a shared workspace.

## FAQ

**Is Notion AI free?**
Limited AI features are available on the free plan (20 AI responses). Full AI access — including AI Agents, unlimited Q&A, and the writing assistant — requires the Business plan at £16/user/month.

**Is Notion AI worth £16/user/month?**
For teams that already use Notion as their primary workspace, yes — the contextual intelligence and autonomous agents save significant time. For individual users or teams that use Notion lightly, it's overpriced compared to standalone AI tools.

**Can Notion AI search my whole workspace?**
Yes. Workspace Q&A (Ask Notion) searches across all pages, databases, and shared content in your workspace, providing answers with source citations.

**What can Notion AI Agents do?**
AI Agents can autonomously execute multi-step tasks for up to 20 minutes — building project plans, compiling feedback from multiple docs, updating database entries at scale, and drafting follow-ups based on meeting notes.

**Is Notion AI better than ChatGPT?**
For workspace-specific tasks (searching your docs, summarising your meeting notes), yes. For general writing, creative tasks, and broad AI capabilities, ChatGPT is more powerful and cheaper.
