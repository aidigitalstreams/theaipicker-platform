## What Is Otter.ai?

Otter.ai is an AI-powered meeting assistant. Its OtterPilot bot joins Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams meetings, records audio, produces a live transcript as the meeting happens, generates a post-meeting summary, extracts action items, and gives users an AI chat interface for asking follow-up questions about what was said and by whom.

The product was one of the first in the category and has benefited from a long development runway. It is available on the web, iOS, and Android, with mobile apps that are usable as standalone voice recorders (capture a conversation, interview, or lecture and get a transcript without a meeting platform involved).

Otter's positioning through 2024-2025 has shifted from "transcription tool" to "AI meeting assistant that works across your meeting stack" — the bot-based joiner, the chat, the action-item workflow, and the CRM integrations are the features that differentiate it from pure transcription products.

## Key Features

**OtterPilot.** Otter's meeting bot. Joins Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams meetings from your calendar, records, transcribes in real time, and produces a summary + action items when the meeting ends. Works whether the user attends or not — OtterPilot can cover a meeting on your behalf and deliver the notes afterward.

**Live transcription.** Transcripts appear during the meeting, not only after. Accuracy on clear English meeting audio is consistently strong in community feedback. Speaker identification works once voice profiles are trained.

**Otter AI Chat.** Ask questions about a meeting after it ends — "what decisions were made?", "what did Sarah commit to?", "summarise the pricing discussion". Useful for meetings the user attended and for meetings they missed.

**Action-item extraction.** Automatically surfaces tasks and owners from the transcript. Quality is good on clearly worded commitments; less reliable on vague or conversational wording, which is a category-wide limitation rather than Otter-specific.

**Integrations.** Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Notion, and Zapier for broader connectivity. Meeting output can be pushed to CRM or documentation tools automatically.

**Mobile app.** iOS and Android apps that work as standalone recorders (great for interviews, lectures, field conversations) as well as joining or reviewing meetings.

**Privacy and compliance.** SOC 2 Type 2, GDPR compliant, HIPAA-configurable on Enterprise. Retention controls are configurable at the Business and Enterprise tiers.

## What We Liked

- The most polished end-to-end UX in the category — web, iOS, and Android all feel like mature, well-designed products.
- Live transcription during meetings is genuinely useful for review-while-you-listen, note-checking, and deaf / hard-of-hearing participants.
- OtterPilot autopilot (Business tier) — the bot can cover meetings without the user attending at all.
- Otter AI Chat is a meaningful capability for reviewing missed or long meetings without re-reading the whole transcript.
- Integration coverage (Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Notion) is mature.
- Mobile recorder use case is a differentiator — not every competitor has a polished standalone recording app.

## What We Didn't Like

- Business tier at $30/user/month is the most expensive mainstream option in the category — Fireflies Business is $19/user/month and Fathom Team is $32/user/month with higher AI usage included.
- Action-item extraction lags community expectations on vague or conversational wording (a category-wide issue, not Otter-specific, but worth noting).
- Language support beyond English is narrower than some competitors — Spanish and French are covered but other European and Asian languages are not at parity.
- Free tier's 30-minute per-conversation limit blocks meaningful real-meeting use.
- Summaries are useful but not as structured as Fathom's templated output.
