## What Is Gemini?

Gemini is Google's family of AI models and the consumer chatbot product wrapping them. The current flagship is Gemini 2.5 Pro; Gemini Flash is the faster, cheaper model; Gemini Ultra is positioned for Enterprise Ultra customers. The consumer chatbot is accessible at gemini.google.com and inside Google Workspace apps (Gmail, Docs, Sheets) for Workspace subscribers.

Google's angle is integration. Gemini is not trying to be the broadest standalone chatbot — it is trying to be the AI layer inside the tools Google users already live in. If you use Gmail, Docs, Drive, and Calendar daily, Gemini is meaningfully more useful than a chatbot that sits outside your workflow.

The second angle is context window. Gemini 2.5 Pro exposes context windows measured in millions of tokens — enough to load an entire book, a long meeting transcript, or a large codebase into a single conversation without chunking. This is a genuine technical advantage over the 200k standard of most competitors.

## Key Features

**Deep Google Workspace integration.** Summarise emails in Gmail. Draft responses inside the Gmail compose window. Ask Gemini to summarise a long document inside Docs. Generate a chart from a Sheets dataset. This is where Gemini is most differentiated — the AI is where your work already is.

**Gemini 2.5 Pro and very long context.** The flagship model supports context windows measured in millions of tokens. For long-document analysis, long codebases, long meeting transcripts, this is a meaningful capability beyond most competitors.

**Gems.** Gemini's custom-assistant feature, equivalent to ChatGPT GPTs. Users create tailored assistants with instructions, uploaded knowledge, and tool access. Less mature ecosystem than the GPT Store, but the core feature works.

**Deep Research.** A multi-step research mode where Gemini plans a research trail, gathers sources, and produces a structured long-form report. Competes with Perplexity Pages and ChatGPT Deep Research.

**Image generation (Imagen).** Native image generation inside Gemini Advanced, using Google's Imagen models. Decent quality; not at Midjourney or DALL-E 3's level on aesthetic ceiling.

**Video generation (Veo).** Google's Veo video generation is accessible through Gemini Advanced with limits. Strong raw quality on some tasks; workflow less mature than Runway.

**Voice mode.** Gemini Live (voice conversation) exists but sits behind ChatGPT's Advanced Voice Mode in polish and naturalness.

**Multimodal input.** Text, image, audio, video input. Camera access on mobile for real-time visual queries.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Gemini better than ChatGPT?**
Depends on your workflow. For Google Workspace users, Gemini's integration is a real advantage. For standalone chatbot use, ChatGPT is more polished.

**Is Gemini Advanced worth $19.99/month?**
For Google Workspace users, yes — especially if the 2TB storage bundle replaces a Google One subscription you already pay for. For users outside the Google ecosystem, the value case is weaker.

**Does Gemini train on my conversations?**
Consumer tier trains by default; opt-out in settings. Workspace with Gemini and Enterprise tiers are private.

**Can Gemini generate images and video?**
Yes. Imagen for images; Veo for video. Both inside Gemini Advanced with limits. Quality is good; not category-leading versus dedicated tools.

**What is Gemini Live?**
Gemini's voice-conversation mode. Functional but behind ChatGPT Advanced Voice Mode in naturalness.
