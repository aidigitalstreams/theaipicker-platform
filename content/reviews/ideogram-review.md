## What Is Ideogram?

Ideogram is an AI image generator with a specific technical focus: rendering legible text inside generated images. Other image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion variants) have historically struggled with text — they would produce near-text, garbled letterforms, or misspell brand words. Ideogram was built to solve this problem, and based on community comparative testing, it consistently produces the most readable text of any mainstream generator.

The product is accessible at ideogram.ai as a web app. Generation happens in a browser-native UI with a gallery of previous work, style presets, and aspect ratio controls. Ideogram 2.0 added Canvas (inpainting and outpainting) in 2024, closing a workflow gap against Midjourney and DALL-E 3.

Ideogram's niche is meaningful: logo concepts, poster design, social graphics with overlaid text, marketing visuals, and anywhere brand-correct typography matters. For photorealistic portraits or cinematic scene generation, Midjourney remains stronger — but for "generate a poster with 'SUMMER SALE' as the headline", Ideogram is the right tool.

## Key Features

**Category-leading text rendering.** Type a prompt asking for specific text in the image, and Ideogram produces legible, correctly-spelled output. This is the single feature that defines the product.

**Typography control.** Style prompts around typefaces, weights, and layouts. Ideogram responds more predictably to typographic direction than competitors.

**Style presets.** Range of built-in aesthetic styles — illustrations, photography, 3D, anime, design. Speeds up ideation without requiring long style prompts.

**Aspect ratios.** Multiple aspect ratios (square, 16:9, 9:16, 3:2, 4:5) for social, web, print workflows.

**Canvas (inpainting / outpainting).** Added in Ideogram 2.0. Refine specific regions of an image or extend beyond the original canvas.

**Magic Prompt.** Auto-expansion of short prompts into richer versions. Useful for quick iteration; disable for precise control.

**Commercial licensing included.** Paid-tier outputs are licensed for commercial use by default — meaningful for marketers and designers producing client work.

**Public gallery.** Community gallery shows other users' generations and lets you remix. Useful for learning prompt patterns.
