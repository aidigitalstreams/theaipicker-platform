## What Is Fireflies.ai?

Fireflies.ai is an AI meeting assistant. Its Fred bot joins meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Webex, BlueJeans, Dialpad, Aircall, and other platforms — meaningfully broader platform coverage than Otter or Fathom. Fred records, transcribes, summarises, extracts action items, and makes the meeting searchable through an AskFred chat interface.

The product was founded in 2016 and has grown into one of the most widely adopted meeting assistants for sales and customer-success teams. Its positioning emphasises value-per-dollar and CRM integration depth — Fireflies pushes meeting output directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and Copper with mature field mappings.

Through 2024-2025 Fireflies has added multi-language support (60+ languages), improved summary quality, and tightened AskFred for cross-meeting search. The core pitch has stayed consistent: the same meeting assistant capability as Otter at a lower team-tier cost and with broader platform support.

## Key Features

**Fred meeting bot.** Joins Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Webex, BlueJeans, Dialpad, Aircall, and others. Broadest platform support in the category.

**Multi-language transcription.** 60+ languages supported, the widest coverage among mainstream meeting assistants. Accuracy on non-English meetings is a genuine Fireflies advantage over Otter.

**AskFred.** Ask questions across a single meeting or across multiple meetings ("what has been said about Project X in the last month?"). The cross-meeting search is a standout feature for teams that need institutional memory.

**Action-item extraction.** Detects tasks and commitments automatically. Quality on typical business meetings is good; vague language remains a challenge (category-wide).

**CRM integration depth.** Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Copper. Field-level mappings are more mature than competitors for sales-team workflows.

**Soundbites.** Clip and share specific moments from a meeting — useful for highlighting a customer quote or a pricing objection for team learning.

**Privacy and compliance.** SOC 2 Type 2, GDPR, HIPAA on Enterprise. Retention and access controls are configurable.
