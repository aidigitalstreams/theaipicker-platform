## What Is Speechify?

Speechify is an AI-powered text-to-speech platform with two related but distinct product lines:

1. **Speechify consumer app** — a text-to-speech listening tool that reads web articles, PDFs, books, emails, and documents aloud. Available on iOS, Android, web, and as a Chrome extension. Used by over 30 million people.
2. **Speechify Studio & Voice Cloning** — a creator-focused platform for generating voiceovers and cloning custom voices for commercial use.

The consumer app is where Speechify earns its reputation. It's polished, fast, and genuinely useful for anyone who wants to convert their reading list into a listening list. Features like active text highlighting (syncs the text being read with the audio) and OCR 4.0 (scans physical books or screenshots and converts them to audio with 99.8% claimed accuracy) are category-leading.

Speechify Studio and Voice Cloning are the creator-facing products. They're good, but they're competing in a tougher market against ElevenLabs, Murf, Lovo, and Play.ht — and they don't top the category on any single dimension.

## Key Features

### Premium Voice Library

Speechify Premium includes 1,000+ voices across 60+ languages. Voice quality is strong — user reviews consistently describe the premium voices as among the most human-like available, with proper emotional pacing and natural pauses. The library is smaller than ElevenLabs' 5,000+ but larger than Murf's 200+.

### Voice Cloning with Identity Locking

Voice cloning requires only a 20-second sample. User reports frequently describe the resulting clones as "scarily lifelike." The 2026 update introduced **Identity Locking** — cloned voices can't be used without biometric verification. This is a meaningful security feature in an era of AI voice deepfake concerns and is rarer than it should be among voice-cloning platforms.

### Active Text Highlighting

When Speechify reads text aloud, the word being spoken is highlighted live in the interface. This is transformative for learners, users with dyslexia, and anyone who learns better with simultaneous visual and auditory input. No other tool in this comparison set does it as well.

### OCR 4.0 for Physical Text

Point your phone camera at a book, article, or document and Speechify converts it to audio — with a claimed 99.8% accuracy on text recognition. For readers, this is a killer feature. For creators, it's irrelevant.

### Multi-Platform Support

Chrome extension, iOS app, Android app, web app, and desktop integrations. Speechify works everywhere you read. This ubiquity is part of why the consumer product dominates its category.

### Offline Listening

Downloaded content can be listened to without an internet connection. Useful for commutes, flights, and anywhere signal is unreliable.

## What We Didn't Like

**Commercial rights are not included on Premium.** This is a repeated friction point in user reviews — subscribers assume Premium includes commercial use and discover later that it doesn't. Clearer upfront communication would fix this.

**Studio doesn't clearly beat creator-first competitors.** At $288/year, Speechify Studio competes with ElevenLabs ($264/year), Murf ($228-348/year), and Lovo ($300/year) — all of which have stronger positioning for creator workflows. Speechify Studio works but doesn't lead the category on any single dimension.

**Billing and cancellation complaints are common.** Multiple user reviews flag difficulty cancelling or surprise renewals. Not universal, but frequent enough to warrant caution. Review the cancellation terms before subscribing.

**Limited video integration.** No built-in video editor, no native presentation integrations. For creators producing video content, Lovo and Murf offer more complete workflows.

**Interface bias toward consumers.** If you're a creator, you'll frequently run into features designed for listeners that aren't relevant to voiceover production.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Speechify worth $139/year?

For listeners — yes. The consumer app is one of the best AI tools you can subscribe to, and $139/year works out to under $12/month. For creators, Speechify Studio at $288/year is less clear-cut — ElevenLabs and Lovo offer stronger creator-focused workflows at similar price points.

### Does Speechify Premium include commercial rights?

No. Premium is for personal listening use only. Commercial use requires Speechify Studio or a specific voice cloning commercial licence. This is one of the most common points of confusion in user reviews — check the licence terms before creating commercial content.

### How accurate is Speechify's voice cloning?

User reports consistently describe the results from a 20-second sample as highly lifelike. The 2026 Identity Locking update requires biometric verification before a cloned voice can be used, which is a meaningful security improvement over most competitors.

### Is Speechify better than ElevenLabs?

For listening to content, yes — Speechify's consumer app is more polished and feature-rich. For creating voiceovers, no — ElevenLabs produces more expressive output and offers better value on voice cloning. They're effectively different products competing for overlapping audiences.

### Can I cancel Speechify easily?

User reviews frequently flag cancellation friction and surprise renewals. The process is possible but takes attention. Read the cancellation terms before subscribing and note the billing date.
