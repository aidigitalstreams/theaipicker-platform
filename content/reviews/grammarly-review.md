## What Is Grammarly?

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that checks grammar, spelling, punctuation, style, and tone in real-time across virtually any application. Founded in 2009, it has grown into the world's most widely-used writing tool with over 30 million daily active users. The 2025 rebrand from "Premium" to "Pro" coincided with a major expansion of AI capabilities under the GrammarlyGO suite.

What differentiates Grammarly from AI content generators like Jasper or Copy.ai is its role as a writing companion rather than a content creator. Grammarly works alongside you as you write — in your email, your document, your social media post — correcting errors, suggesting improvements, and now offering AI-powered rewrites and generation through GrammarlyGO. It doesn't replace the writer; it makes the writer better.

## Key Features

**Grammar, Spelling & Punctuation.** The foundation. Grammarly catches errors that spell-checkers miss — comma splices, subject-verb disagreements, misplaced modifiers, incorrect tenses. Independent testing puts accuracy between 93-98%, the highest in the category. Real-time underlining with explanations for each correction helps users learn, not just fix.

**GrammarlyGO.** The AI writing suite introduced in 2023 and expanded significantly since. Includes paraphrasing, full-sentence rewrites, tone adjustment, text generation from prompts, expert review suggestions, and a reader reactions analysis that predicts how your text will be perceived. Pro users get 2,000 AI prompts per month (20x the Free plan's 100).

**Tone Detection.** Analyses your text and labels the tone — confident, friendly, formal, concerned, etc. Useful for email communication where tone mismatches can cause problems. The suggestions are practical: "This might come across as demanding. Consider softening with..."

**Plagiarism Checker.** Scans text against billions of web pages. Pro-only. Functional for catching accidental similarity but less thorough than dedicated tools like Turnitin.

**Brand Tones & Style Guides (Business).** Business plan users can define custom writing rules, brand voice guidelines, and terminology preferences that apply across the organisation. This turns Grammarly from a personal tool into an enterprise writing standard.

**Cross-Platform Integration.** Works in 1 million+ applications — Gmail, Slack, Google Docs, Microsoft Word, social media platforms, CMS editors, and any web-based text field via the browser extension. Also available as a desktop app, mobile keyboard, and API.

## FAQ

**Is Grammarly Free good enough?**
For basic grammar and spelling checks, yes. The free plan catches most common errors and includes 100 AI prompts per month. Upgrade to Pro if you need the plagiarism checker, advanced style suggestions, or 2,000 AI prompts.

**Is Grammarly worth paying for?**
For daily professional writers, yes — the advanced grammar suggestions, GrammarlyGO features, and plagiarism checker justify £12/month. For occasional writers, the free plan is likely sufficient.

**Does Grammarly work with Google Docs?**
Yes. The browser extension integrates directly with Google Docs, providing real-time grammar checking, style suggestions, and GrammarlyGO features within the document editor.

**Is Grammarly better than ChatGPT for writing?**
They serve different purposes. Grammarly excels at editing and improving text you've already written, with superior grammar checking and cross-platform integration. ChatGPT excels at generating content from prompts. Many writers use both — ChatGPT to draft, Grammarly to polish.

**Does Grammarly sell your data?**
Grammarly states it does not sell user data. Text is processed to provide suggestions and is encrypted in transit. Enterprise and Business plans offer additional data controls and compliance certifications.
