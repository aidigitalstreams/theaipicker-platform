## What Is Canva AI?

Canva is a cloud-based design platform used by over 190 million people worldwide for creating social media graphics, presentations, marketing materials, videos, and more. Magic Studio is Canva's AI suite — a collection of 25+ AI-powered tools integrated directly into the Canva editor. Launched in late 2023 and continuously expanded since, Magic Studio transforms Canva from a template-based design tool into an AI-assisted design production system.

What differentiates Canva AI from dedicated image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E, Recraft) is its integration into a complete design workflow. Users don't generate an image and then import it into a design tool — they generate, edit, apply brand guidelines, add text, and export, all in one place. This end-to-end workflow makes Canva AI uniquely appealing to non-designers, small business owners, and marketing teams who need professional-looking output without professional design skills.

## Key Features

**Magic Design.** Generate complete design sets — social media posts, presentations, documents, videos — from a text prompt or uploaded file. The AI creates multiple variations in different styles, which can be customised in the editor. Brand Kit integration means generated designs automatically use your colours, fonts, and logos.

**Dream Lab.** Canva's text-to-image generator, powered by multiple models. Generate custom images from text descriptions directly within the editor. Quality is functional for design purposes — good enough for social media and marketing materials, but a step behind dedicated generators for fine art or hyper-realistic output.

**Magic Write.** AI text generation for social media copy, presentation slides, marketing blurbs, and documents. Powered by GPT integration. Useful for generating first-draft copy within designs.

**Magic Edit.** Select an area of any image and describe what you want to change. Useful for quick edits — removing objects, changing backgrounds, adjusting elements — without leaving Canva.

**Magic Eraser & Background Remover.** One-click background removal and object erasure. Staple features that work reliably and save significant time compared to manual editing.

**Magic Expand.** Extend images beyond their original boundaries using AI. Useful for adapting images to different aspect ratios without cropping important content.

**Brand Kit Integration.** This is Canva AI's strongest differentiator. Define brand colours, fonts, logos, and guidelines, and Magic Design applies them intelligently to AI-generated designs. Based on research, this consistently saves 10-15 minutes per project compared to manual brand application.

## FAQ

**Is Canva AI free?**
Canva has a free plan with limited AI features. Full Magic Studio access including Dream Lab, Magic Edit, and 500 AI credits requires Canva Pro (£10/month annually).

**How many AI credits does Canva Pro include?**
500 per month, shared across all AI features. A batch of Dream Lab images uses 100-150 credits. Credits do not carry over.

**Is Canva better than Midjourney for AI images?**
No. Midjourney produces significantly higher-quality AI images. Canva's advantage is the integrated design workflow — generate, edit, brand, and export in one place. If image quality is the priority, use Midjourney.

**Can I use Canva AI for commercial projects?**
Yes, on Pro and above. All AI-generated content in Canva Pro comes with a commercial licence.

**Does Canva AI work on mobile?**
Yes. The Canva mobile app includes Magic Studio features, though the experience is best on desktop or tablet.
