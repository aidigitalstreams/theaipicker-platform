## What Is QuillBot?

QuillBot is an AI-powered paraphrasing and writing refinement tool that helps users rephrase text while preserving the original meaning. Acquired by Course Hero (now part of Learneo) in 2023, it has grown from a simple paraphrase-and-replace tool into a broader writing suite that includes grammar checking, summarisation, plagiarism detection, citation generation, and the new Flow workspace for end-to-end writing.

What makes QuillBot distinct from tools like Jasper, Copy.ai, or Writesonic is its focus. Those tools generate content from prompts. QuillBot takes existing text and makes it better — clearer, more concise, differently worded, or adapted for a specific tone. This makes it popular with students refining essays, ESL writers improving fluency, content editors polishing drafts, and professionals who need to rephrase source material.

## Key Features

**Paraphraser with 10 Modes.** The core feature. Standard, Fluency, Formal, Simple, Creative, Expand, Shorten, Academic, Custom, and AI Humaniser modes. Each transforms text differently — Standard preserves meaning with light rephrasing, Creative takes more liberties, Academic adjusts for scholarly tone. Users can control the "synonym slider" to adjust how aggressively the tool changes wording.

**QuillBot Flow.** A full writing workspace launched in 2025 that combines drafting, outlining, paraphrasing, and research in a single interface. Users can draft content from scratch, generate outlines, paraphrase sections, and pull research sources without switching tabs. This extends QuillBot from a single-purpose tool into a lightweight writing environment.

**Grammar Checker.** Real-time grammar, punctuation, and style correction. Premium unlocks full feedback including sentence structure suggestions, tone adjustments, and clarity improvements. Competitive with Grammarly's core grammar features, though less comprehensive on style guidance.

**Summariser.** Condenses long text into key points or paragraph form. Useful for quickly extracting the core message from research papers, articles, or meeting notes. Premium increases the length limits significantly.

**Plagiarism Checker.** Scans text against web sources to identify potential plagiarism. Available on Premium. Functional but less comprehensive than dedicated tools like Turnitin or Copyscape.

**Citation Generator.** Generates citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, and other formats from URLs, DOIs, or manual entry. Primarily useful for academic writing.

**AI Humaniser.** Attempts to rephrase AI-generated text to avoid detection by AI content detectors. Based on independent testing, it achieves approximately a 42% bypass rate across major detectors — meaning it works less than half the time.

## FAQ

**Is QuillBot free?**
Yes, but the free plan is very limited — 125-word paraphrase cap, basic grammar checking, and restricted summariser length. Most regular users will need Premium (from £3.33/month annually).

**Can QuillBot bypass AI detectors?**
The AI Humaniser mode attempts this but achieves only about a 42% bypass rate across major detectors based on independent testing. It's unreliable as a primary strategy for avoiding AI detection.

**Is QuillBot considered plagiarism?**
Using QuillBot to paraphrase properly attributed source material is not plagiarism. Using it to disguise copied text without attribution is. The tool itself is neutral — how it's used determines the ethical question. Most academic institutions consider QuillBot an acceptable editing aid when used transparently.

**How does QuillBot compare to Grammarly?**
Grammarly is a broader writing assistant with superior grammar checking, style guidance, and GrammarlyGO AI features. QuillBot is a focused paraphrasing tool with a lower price point. They serve different primary needs — Grammarly for writing quality, QuillBot for rewording.

**Does QuillBot work with Google Docs?**
Yes. The Chrome extension integrates directly with Google Docs, allowing in-document paraphrasing and grammar checking without leaving the document.
