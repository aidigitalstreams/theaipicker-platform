## What Is Fathom?

Fathom is an AI meeting assistant for Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Instead of a traditional bot-joiner approach, Fathom uses a combination of native platform integrations and lightweight capture — particularly tight integration with Zoom through a Chrome extension and native flow.

The product records, transcribes, summarises, extracts action items, and makes meetings searchable through an AI chat. Its differentiation is two-fold: the free tier is genuinely generous (unlimited recordings, transcripts, and summaries for individuals) and the UX is consistently praised in community feedback as the most polished in the category.

Fathom has grown through 2023-2025 on the strength of word-of-mouth — it is the meeting assistant that individuals recommend to teammates, and teams that adopt it tend to keep it. That organic growth trajectory is why it punches above its weight against incumbent Otter and value-leader Fireflies.

## Key Features

**Unlimited free tier.** Individuals can record, transcribe, and summarise unlimited meetings on Zoom, Google Meet, and MS Teams at no cost. No time limits, no meeting count limits. This is the most generous offer in the category.

**Polished meeting UX.** Community feedback consistently rates Fathom as the most user-friendly product in the category — fast capture, fast post-meeting processing, clean summary layouts.

**Template-driven summaries.** Fathom's summaries follow structured templates (key points, decisions, action items) rather than narrative paragraphs. This makes them scan-readable in a way some competitors' summaries are not.

**Action-item extraction with ownership.** Detects action items and surfaces owners. Quality is competitive with Otter and Fireflies; consistency on clearly worded commitments is strong.

**AI chat ("Ask Fathom").** Query meetings after the fact. Works on single meetings; cross-meeting search is available on Team tier.

**Instant clip sharing.** Pull a specific moment from a meeting as a shareable clip — useful for highlighting customer quotes or objection handling.

**CRM integrations (Premium / Team).** HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Notion, Asana, and Zapier. Integration depth is solid though narrower than Fireflies.

**Privacy posture.** SOC 2 Type 2, GDPR compliant. Retention controls available on team tiers.
