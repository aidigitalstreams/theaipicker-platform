## What Is Claude?

Claude is Anthropic's consumer-facing chatbot product, built on the Claude family of large language models — Opus for heavy reasoning, Sonnet for balanced daily use, and Haiku for fast, cheap, lightweight tasks. The current generation at time of writing is Claude Opus 4.6, Sonnet 4.6, and Haiku 4.5.

Anthropic's positioning has been deliberately narrower than OpenAI's. The product focuses on conversational quality, long-form writing, reasoning under complex instructions, and coding. Claude has less multimodal sprawl than ChatGPT — there is no native image generator inside Claude, no video generation equivalent to Sora, and voice mode is less developed. The trade-off is depth: for the use cases Claude does cover, practitioner feedback consistently rates output quality at the top of the category.

## Key Features

**The Claude model family.** Opus is the flagship, used when quality matters most. Sonnet is the default for daily use — fast enough for conversation, strong enough for most tasks. Haiku is for high-volume or latency-sensitive work.

**Projects.** A workspace feature for grouping related conversations, uploaded documents, and shared context. Strong for anyone working on a sustained piece of work — a book, a codebase, a long research project — where the AI needs persistent context.

**Artifacts.** A side-panel surface where Claude renders code, documents, or visual content that persists across a conversation. Users edit and iterate on artifacts without losing the rest of the chat. Strong for writing and code workflows.

**Computer Use and Agent SDK.** Claude can operate a computer (click, type, navigate) or be integrated into custom agents via the Claude Agent SDK. This is the capability that power users and developers increasingly build around.

**Claude Code.** A terminal-based coding agent built on Claude, popular with developers who prefer CLI workflows over IDE integrations. Has its own review in our [AI Coding Assistants](/rankings/ai-coding-assistants/) category.

**Long context windows.** Claude 4.6 models offer 200k-1M token context depending on model and tier, suitable for long-document analysis and extended multi-turn technical work.

**File upload.** PDFs, images, spreadsheets, code files — read and reasoned about directly.

**What Claude does not have (that ChatGPT does).** Native image generation, native video generation, a polished advanced voice mode equivalent. Voice mode is available but markedly less developed than ChatGPT's.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Claude better than ChatGPT?**
For long-form writing and reasoning-heavy work, user feedback consistently favours Claude. For breadth of capability (voice, image, video generation), ChatGPT is more complete. Many professionals pay for both.

**What is the difference between Claude Opus, Sonnet, and Haiku?**
Opus is the highest-quality, slowest, most expensive. Sonnet is the balanced default — fast enough for conversation, strong on most tasks. Haiku is the fastest and cheapest, used for high-volume or latency-sensitive work.

**Is Claude Pro worth $20/month?**
For regular users, yes. The step from free (Sonnet with caps) to Pro (Opus access, Projects, Artifacts, higher caps) is meaningful.

**Does Claude train on my conversations?**
Free and Pro tiers may train by default depending on region and settings; opt-out is available. Team and Enterprise tiers do not train on user data by default.

**What is Claude Code?**
A terminal-based coding agent built on Claude for developers who work in CLI. See our full [Claude Code review](/claude-code-review/).
