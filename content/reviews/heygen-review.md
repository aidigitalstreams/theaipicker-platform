## What Is HeyGen?

HeyGen is an AI video platform that generates realistic avatar-led videos from text scripts. Users type (or paste) a script, pick an avatar and voice, and HeyGen produces a finished video of that avatar speaking the script — with synchronised lip movements, natural gestures, and accurate intonation.

The 2026 platform runs on its **Avatar IV** model, which is a significant step change from earlier versions. Where previous AI avatars looked "obviously AI" under close inspection — slightly off eye movements, stilted gestures, uncanny mouth shapes — Avatar IV output often passes as real human video on first watch. HeyGen supports 175+ languages and dialects, offers custom avatar creation from a photo and voice sample, and is widely used for marketing video, product demos, training content, and social-first creator content.

HeyGen competes directly with Synthesia (enterprise-focused), Colossyan, and a growing field of avatar video tools. Its pitch is clear: highest realism in the category, flexible credit-based pricing, accessible custom avatars.

## Key Features

### Avatar IV — The Realism Leap

The Avatar IV model is HeyGen's 2026 flagship. User reviews and independent comparisons consistently place it ahead of Synthesia's current avatars for naturalness. The differences that matter: eye contact and blink patterns, gesture variety and fluidity, micro-expressions during speech, and mouth shape accuracy.

For client-facing marketing content or social media where the avatar is the hero, this quality gap is worth paying for. For compliance-heavy training videos where avatar realism is secondary to process and security, Synthesia's stability may matter more.

### 175+ Languages and Dialects

HeyGen supports 175+ languages and regional dialects — ahead of Synthesia's 140+. For international creators or global marketing teams, this coverage is meaningful. Voice and avatar can be mixed-and-matched across language boundaries.

### Custom Avatar Creation

HeyGen's custom avatar feature lets users create a digital avatar based on a photo and voice sample. This is available on paid plans starting around $99/month for the custom avatar tier — accessible, but not cheap. Custom avatars are genuinely useful for brand-consistent content where a real founder or presenter wants to appear across hundreds of pieces without shooting each one.

The process typically requires a short video sample of the person speaking, which HeyGen uses to train a dedicated avatar model. Once trained, that avatar can be driven by any script.

### Large Stock Avatar Library

Hundreds of stock avatars covering varied demographics, professional contexts, and styles. For creators who don't need a custom avatar, the stock library is more than sufficient for most marketing and social use cases.

### Voice Cloning

HeyGen includes voice cloning alongside custom avatars. For creators who want the full "digital twin" experience — their avatar, their voice, producing content 24/7 — this is the complete package.

### Translations and Multilingual Video

Generate the same video in multiple languages without re-recording. HeyGen handles the lip-sync re-mapping automatically, so the avatar appears to genuinely speak each language rather than having dubbed audio over mismatched mouth movements. This is one of the strongest practical applications of AI avatar video.
