## What Is Clearscope?

Clearscope is a content optimisation platform that grades your writing against the top-ranking content for any keyword. It uses NLP and IBM Watson to identify the semantic keywords and topics your content should cover, then gives you a simple letter grade (F to A++) that reflects how comprehensively your content addresses the topic compared to what is currently ranking.

The platform's philosophy is simplicity. While Surfer offers 500+ signals and MarketMuse offers five interconnected applications, Clearscope focuses on one thing: helping you write content that covers the topic thoroughly. The grading system is intuitive enough that any writer can understand and use it without SEO expertise.

## Key Features

**Content grading.** Real-time letter grades (F to A++) based on topical coverage versus competitors. The grading is based on semantic analysis, not keyword density — Clearscope identifies related terms, entities, and concepts that top-ranking content includes. This is the core feature and it works well.

**Semantic keyword analysis.** Uses NLP and IBM Watson to surface semantically related terms — not just keyword variations, but the concepts and entities that Google expects comprehensive content to address. The semantic grouping feature links related terms together to help writers understand topic relationships.

**AI Visibility tracking.** The "Expand" feature monitors how ChatGPT and Gemini reference your brand and content. This is a newer addition, similar to Surfer's AI Tracker but less mature.

**Internal linking suggestions.** Real-time suggestions for internal links as you write, based on your existing content. This helps build site architecture without requiring a separate linking tool.

**Native integrations.** Google Docs add-on and WordPress plugin bring full Clearscope functionality into the writing environments teams already use. This is a genuine advantage — writers never need to leave their preferred editor.

**Unlimited team seats.** All plans include unlimited user accounts and projects. No per-seat pricing. For teams, this is a significant cost advantage over competitors that charge per seat.

## What We Liked

The simplicity is the killer feature. In a category where tools like Surfer and MarketMuse can feel overwhelming, Clearscope strips content optimisation down to its essence: write, check your grade, fill the gaps, repeat. Any writer can use it — no SEO training required. For content teams where the writers are not SEO specialists, this ease of adoption is worth paying for.

Unlimited team seats on all plans make the maths work for larger teams. At £189/month for unlimited users, a 10-person content team pays £18.90 per user — significantly cheaper than Surfer's per-seat model at scale. This pricing structure rewards team adoption.

The Google Docs and WordPress integrations are genuinely native, not bolt-on. Writers never leave their preferred editor, which means they actually use the tool instead of treating it as an extra step.

## What We Didn't Like

The entry price is the highest in the category. £189/month with no free plan and no free trial creates a significant trust barrier. Prospects must commit to a sales demo and then a paid plan without hands-on evaluation. In a market where Surfer offers a money-back guarantee and MarketMuse offers a free tier, this is a competitive weakness.

Analytical depth is less than Surfer. Clearscope does not offer a SERP Analyzer with 500+ signals, topic clustering, or content audit features. If you want to understand why top-ranking pages rank — not just what topics they cover — Surfer provides deeper analysis.

50 reports per month on the Essentials plan may not be enough for teams publishing at high volume. Additional reports require upgrading to Business at £399/month — a steep jump.
